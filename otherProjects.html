design your sound

(2023)

DESIGN YOUR SOUND is a web application made as an academic work for a master course.

It is a tool created with the aim of simplify the sound design. It is designed for those who want to find a sound file online and manipulate it in a simple and quick way.

All the sounds provided by DESIGN YOUR SOUND are part of the Freesound API. DESIGN YOUR SOUND only provides 0-Common Licenses sounds, which means all the soundfiles used inside this application are copyrights free.
