à mesa

(2025; resident artist)

À Mesa is a collective artistic project that combines listening practices, contemporary music creation and community gatherings. Through residencies and local interventions, it proposes a sensitive and situated approach to art, directly connected to the territory and its people

The first edition of the project took place at Celorico de Basto in April 2025, around a table full of food, traditions, legends and superstitions

(Artistic direction: Patrícia Pinheiro // Artists in residency: Ema Ferreira, Inês Luzio, Patrícia Pinheiro and Teresa Costa // Support to the project: TONALI )

Improvisa – Life in Motion

(2021/22; resident artist)

IMPROVISA - Life in Motion project is a collaboration between 5 European organizations.The project explores the potential of the audio-visual improvisation driven by mobile technologies, through participatory experiences aimed to engage audiences with difficult access to culture, such as migrants and inmates.

As a resident artist, my job was to create a workshop that made use of the museums' collections and the Improvisa App, as well as to implement it in the different museums. "BEAUTIFUL NOISES" was a workshop design to experiment the human voice as a medium for creating music and sounds, using it in an extended way. By combining sound samples and visuals with the IMPROVISA App, participants were encouraged to create their compositions and feel how our spoken, whispered and onomatopoeic voices can be part of an artistic process.
